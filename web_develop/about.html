Welcome to ZZEROH
We provide an open access platform for easy web publishing of literary works and scientific research to all.
Our Goals
At ZZEROH we want to make Web-Publishing easy and accessible to all. Whether you are a budding fiction writer, student, or career researcher, we strive to bring you the resources to share your work with the world.
Contribute
We accept all literary works of writing regardless of skill level, genre, topic or length to our literary library. We also accept all scientific research new or old regardless of H index.
In The Works
Our website is constantly being updated and new features are added to enhance your publishing experience and make the publishing process easier. If you have suggestions regarding future updates reach out to us on our contact page.
